Long form

This section exists to ensure the scroll stack feels weighty and consistent across large vertical ranges. In week 2 we’ll wire the router, store, and navigation shell.

Operational seriousness

The interface should feel like it belongs in a national exam control room: decisive layouts, disciplined typography, and carefully rationed ornamentation.

Information density

Prioritise scanability. Use mono for identifiers and hashes. Keep surfaces warm and light; reserve saturation for state.

Motion restraint

Animate transforms and opacity only. Scroll-linked motion must be GSAP + ScrollTrigger; UI state changes can be CSS.
